Disposal and Recycling

This product contains electronic components and a lithium battery (for RTC). Must be disposed of in accordance with WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) regulations.

Disposal Instructions:

  • Do NOT dispose in household waste
  • Return to authorized e-waste collection point
  • Remove SIM card before disposal
  • Contact local waste management for recycling options
