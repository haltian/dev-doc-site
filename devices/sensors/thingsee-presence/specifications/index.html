Environmental Considerations

Temperature Performance:

  • Optimal detection: 15-25°C ambient temperature
  • Reduced reliability when ambient approaches body temperature (~36°C)
  • Cold environments may require lower detection threshold
  • Avoid locations with rapid temperature changes

Humidity Impact:

  • Non-condensing humidity only
  • Condensation will damage electronics
  • High humidity (>90%) may affect adhesive tape
  • Indoor use recommended

Detection Characteristics

What Can Be Detected:

  • ✅ Walking humans (normal speed)
  • ✅ Running humans
  • ✅ Large animals (dogs, etc.)
  • ✅ Multiple people in group

What Cannot Be Reliably Detected:

  • ❌ Stationary persons
  • ❌ Very slow movement (<0.1 m/s)
  • ❌ Small animals (cats, rodents)
  • ❌ Objects at same temperature as environment
