Environmental Protection

IP68 certification:

  • Dust ingress: Complete protection against dust (6 rating)
  • Water ingress: Protected against continuous immersion up to 1.5m depth (8 rating)
  • Real-world use: Outdoor installation, exposed to rain, snow, ice, water wash-down

MIL-STD-810H tested:

  • Drop test: Survives installation handling
  • Vibration: Tolerates loading dock vibrations, vehicle traffic
  • Thermal cycling: Rapid temperature changes
  • Humidity: High humidity environments
  • Shock: Impact resistance
Industrial Durability

MIL-STD-810H is a rigorous U.S. military testing standard. Passing MIL-STD-810H confirms the sensor can operate in harsh industrial and outdoor environments.

Detection Cone Coverage

At different distances:

  • At 2m (parking): ~60 cm width coverage
  • At 5m (loading dock): ~150 cm width coverage
  • At 10m (maximum): ~300 cm width coverage

Calculation: Width ≈ 2 × distance × tan(8.5°)

Battery Life Estimates

Based on measurement intervals:

ConfigurationMeasurement IntervalExpected Battery Life
Parking default30 seconds~10 years
Loading dock default60 seconds~10+ years
Low-power custom5 minutes15+ years (estimated)
Heavy router usage(Varies - relaying packets)7-10 years

Factors affecting battery life:

  • Measurement frequency (more measurements = faster drain)
  • Transmission frequency (more reports = faster drain)
  • Mesh network role (routers relay packets, consume more power)
  • Environmental temperature (extreme cold reduces capacity)
End of Life

Battery is non-replaceable. When battery depleted (after ~10 years), sensor has reached end of life and must be replaced with new unit.

Data Transmission

Payload structure (example):

{
  "occupied": true,
  "distance": 1.8,
  "battery": 95,
  "timestamp": "2025-01-14T10:30:00Z"
}

Transmission behavior:

  • State change: Report when occupied ↔ unoccupied changes
  • Periodic: Report even if state unchanged (e.g., hourly)
  • Battery optimized: Minimal transmissions when state stable

Comparison to Alternative Technologies

RADAR vs. Other Parking Sensors

TechnologyRangeOutdoorBattery LifeCostAccuracy
60GHz RADAR (Haltian)10cm-10m✅ Yes (IP68)10 years$$$±5 cm
Ultrasonic10cm-4m⚠️ Limited (moisture issues)5-7 years$$±2 cm
Infrared10cm-2m❌ No (sunlight interference)5 years$±5 cm
Magnetometer (ground)N/A (presence)✅ Yes10 years$Binary only
Camera/VisionVaries✅ Yes (with housing)Wired/solar$$$$High (AI-based)

RADAR advantages:

  • Works in all weather (rain, snow, fog)
  • Outdoor durability (IP68, MIL-STD-810H)
  • Long range (up to 10m)
  • No sunlight/lighting dependency
  • 10-year battery life

RADAR trade-offs:

  • Higher cost than ultrasonic/infrared
  • Binary detection only (no vehicle identification)
  • Requires proper aiming (17° cone)
