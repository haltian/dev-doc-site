Specifications

Complete technical specifications for Thingsee COUNT people counting sensor

Product Overview

Thingsee COUNT is a USB-powered people counting sensor using Time-of-Flight (ToF) laser technology for directional counting in doorways and passageways.

Primary features:

  • Time-of-Flight (ToF) laser distance measurement
  • Directional people counting (entries and exits)
  • USB 5V powered (continuous power required, no battery)
  • Wirepas Mesh network connectivity
  • Maximum 1000mm passageway width
  • Indoor use, cradle mounting system

Detection Technology

Time-of-Flight (ToF) Sensor

Technology:

  • Type: ToF laser distance measurement
  • Laser class: Class 1 laser (eye-safe under normal use)
  • Wavelength: Infrared (IR) laser (typically 850-940nm, invisible to human eye)
  • Detection principle: Measures time for laser pulse to reflect off objects (people, floor)
  • Distance accuracy: ±1-5% typical (millimeter precision)
  • Detection range: 0-2100mm vertical (floor to ceiling)

Advantages:

  • Works in darkness (laser provides own illumination)
  • Temperature independent (measures distance, not heat)
  • High precision (millimeter-level accuracy)
  • Simultaneous passage detection (2-3 people side-by-side)

Limitations:

  • Bright sunlight interference (laser wavelength affected)
  • Certain artificial lighting interference (incandescent, fluorescent, halogen)

Physical Specifications

Dimensions and Weight

Sensor unit (with cradle):

  • Length: Approximately 80mm
  • Width: Approximately 60mm
  • Depth: Approximately 40mm (from ceiling/mounting surface)
  • Weight: Approximately 100g (sensor + cradle + cable clamp)

Cradle mounting system:

  • Cradle: Plastic housing for sensor attachment
  • Cable clamp: Integrated cable management for USB cable strain relief
  • Sensor unit: Detachable from cradle (snap-in design with springs)

Installation Requirements

Passageway dimensions:

  • Maximum width: 1000mm (1 meter)
  • Maximum height: 2100mm (2.1 meters, floor to ceiling)
  • Recommended installation height: 230cm from floor

Mounting methods:

  • Tape: 3M adhesive tape on attachment plate accessory (smooth surfaces)
  • Screw: Permanent screw installation with Torx screwdriver (included)

Mounting surface:

  • Ceiling, doorframe top, wall (horizontal orientation)
  • Must support ~100g weight (sensor minimal load)
  • Smooth, clean surface recommended for tape installation

Power Specifications

USB Power Requirements

Input power:

  • Voltage: 5V DC (standard USB voltage)
  • Current: Typical ~100-300mA (sensor active continuously)
  • Power consumption: Approximately 0.5-1.5W (typical for ToF sensors)

Power supply:

  • Included: USB wall adapter (5V, ≥500mA)
  • Cable: USB A to micro-USB or USB-C (varies by model version)
  • Cable length: Approximately 2-3 meters (adequate for ceiling-to-outlet routing)

Power continuity requirements:

  • Continuous power: Sensor must remain powered at all times
  • No battery backup: Counter resets to zero if power interrupted
  • UPS recommended: For critical applications where count must survive power outages

Power Outlet Recommendations

Suitable:

  • Dedicated outlet (not shared with high-power devices)
  • Not controlled by light switch or timer
  • Reliable circuit (minimal outages)
  • Accessible for maintenance (disconnecting/reconnecting if needed)

Unsuitable:

  • Switchable outlets (risk of accidental power-off)
  • Outlets on overloaded circuits (voltage fluctuations)
  • Temporary power strips (instability risk)

Network Connectivity

Wirepas Mesh Network

Technology:

  • Protocol: Wirepas Mesh (proprietary wireless mesh networking)
  • Frequency: 2.4 GHz ISM band (same as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth)
  • Range: Up to ~20 meters to gateway (direct line of sight)
    • ~10 meters through walls (varies by construction)
    • Extended via mesh routers (other Wirepas sensors act as relays)

Network behavior:

  • Automatic joining: Sensor automatically joins Wirepas mesh after installation
  • Self-healing: Mesh automatically routes around failed nodes
  • Low power: Optimized for battery devices (COUNT is USB-powered, not power-constrained)

Data Transmission

Measurement data:

  • Values transmitted: Entries count, exits count, timestamp
  • Data format: JSON payloads via Wirepas mesh to gateway
  • Reporting frequency: Configurable (default typically 30 seconds to 5 minutes)
  • Trigger-based reporting: Optional (report every X entries/exits instead of periodic)

Payload example:

{
  "measurementDirectionalMovement": [
    {
      "deviceId": "abc123...",
      "entries": 5,
      "exits": 3,
      "measuredAt": "2026-01-20T14:30:00.000+00:00"
    }
  ]
}

Network requirements:

  • Haltian Wirepas gateway within range (~20m)
  • Or Wirepas routers/repeaters to extend mesh coverage
  • Adequate signal strength (RSSI > -85 dBm recommended)

Environmental Specifications

Operating Conditions

Temperature:

  • Operating range: 0°C to +40°C (recommended, indoor use)
  • Storage range: -10°C to +60°C (when not powered)

Humidity:

  • Operating humidity: 20% to 80% RH (relative humidity), non-condensing
  • Storage humidity: 10% to 90% RH, non-condensing

Atmospheric pressure:

  • Operating: 86 kPa to 106 kPa (equivalent to sea level to ~1500m elevation)

Environmental Protection

Indoor use only:

  • IP rating: Not specified (not designed for outdoor use)
  • No weatherproofing: Not resistant to rain, snow, or extreme moisture
  • Dust sensitivity: ToF sensor lens requires clean environment (dust reduces accuracy)

Suitable environments:

  • Climate-controlled offices, meeting rooms, retail stores
  • Indoor passageways with moderate humidity and temperature
  • Protected from direct sunlight (sun interferes with ToF laser)

Unsuitable environments:

  • Outdoor installations (no weatherproofing)
  • High-moisture areas (risk of condensation on ToF lens)
  • Dusty or industrial environments (lens contamination)
  • Direct sunlight exposure (laser interference)

Lighting Conditions

Optimal Lighting

Works best with:

  • Moderate indoor lighting (office lighting, overhead LEDs)
  • Consistent lighting (not rapidly changing)
  • Indirect sunlight (ambient light through windows, not direct sun)

Lighting Interference

Avoid:

  • Bright sunlight: Direct sunlight on sensor (infrared wavelength interference)
  • Incandescent lamps: Certain wavelengths affect ToF laser accuracy
  • Fluorescent lamps: May interfere with ToF measurements
  • Halogen lamps: High IR output can confuse ToF sensor
  • Rapidly changing light: Flashing lights, strobe effects

Mitigation:

  • Install away from windows with direct sun
  • Use window shades/blinds to reduce sun exposure
  • Choose locations with LED lighting (minimal IR output, compatible with ToF)

Performance Specifications

Detection Accuracy

Counting accuracy:

  • Single-file traffic: > 95% accuracy (one person at a time)
  • Moderate traffic: > 90% accuracy (1-3 people per minute)
  • Busy traffic: > 85% accuracy (2-3 people side-by-side)

Factors affecting accuracy:

  • Passageway width (narrower = better, up to 1000mm max)
  • Lighting conditions (moderate lighting optimal, avoid bright sun)
  • Installation height (230cm recommended for optimal coverage)
  • Walking speed (normal to fast preferred, very slow may be missed)

Simultaneous Passage

Capability:

  • Detects 2-3 people walking side-by-side (within 1000mm width)
  • Higher accuracy than PIR-based sensors for simultaneous passage
  • Very crowded passage (> 3 people simultaneously) may cause undercounting

Measurement Specifications

Count Data

Measurement values:

  • entries: Integer count of people entering (based on direction configuration)
  • exits: Integer count of people exiting (opposite of entry direction)
  • timestamp: ISO 8601 format with timezone (e.g., 2026-01-20T14:30:00.000+00:00)

Counter behavior:

  • Incremental: Counts increment by 1 for each detected passage
  • Reset on power loss: Counter resets to zero if USB power disconnected
  • No negative counts: Entries and exits always ≥ 0

Reporting Behavior

Default:

  • Periodic reporting (e.g., every 30 seconds to 5 minutes, configurable)
  • Transmits cumulative entries and exits since last report

Optional trigger-based:

  • Report when entry or exit count exceeds threshold (e.g., every 10 entries/exits)
  • Contact Haltian support to configure trigger-based reporting

Configuration Specifications

Configurable Parameters

Locally configurable (via Thingsee Toolbox app):

  • Entry direction: IN or OUT (determines which direction is “entry” vs. “exit”)

Remotely configurable (via Haltian support):

  • Reporting interval: How often sensor transmits data (e.g., 30s, 1min, 5min)
  • Count threshold: Trigger-based reporting (e.g., report every X entries/exits)

Not configurable:

  • ToF sensor parameters (factory-calibrated)
  • Wirepas network credentials (pre-provisioned)

When to Choose Thingsee COUNT

Advantages:

  • Higher accuracy (ToF laser precision)
  • Better simultaneous passage detection (2-3 people side-by-side)
  • No battery maintenance (continuous USB power)
  • Temperature independent (works in hot/cold environments)

Choose COUNT when:

  • Power outlet available near installation location
  • Higher accuracy required (retail analytics, critical applications)
  • Simultaneous passage common (busy doorways)
  • Ambient temperature varies widely (ToF unaffected by temp)

Choose ENTRYWAY instead when:

  • No power outlet available (battery-powered preferred)
  • Wider passageways (> 1000mm, up to 1600mm)
  • Counter must survive power outages (battery-backed)
  • Bright sunlight or incompatible lighting (PIR unaffected)

End of Life and Disposal

Product Lifespan

Expected operational lifespan:

  • Sensor electronics: 5-10 years (typical for USB-powered IoT sensors)
  • ToF sensor: Factory-calibrated, no recalibration needed over lifespan
  • Cradle: Durable plastic, 10+ years with proper care

Limitations:

  • USB cable may wear over time (replaceable)
  • Adhesive tape (for tape installation) degrades (~5-10 years, sensor may sag)
  • ToF lens may accumulate dust (clean periodically to maintain accuracy)

Disposal and Recycling

Environmental compliance:

  • RoHS compliant (no hazardous substances)
  • Recyclable electronics (follow local e-waste regulations)

Disposal:

  • Do not dispose in general household waste (electronics recycling required)
  • Contact local e-waste recycling facility
  • Remove USB cable and power adapter (recycle separately if needed)
  • Cradle and sensor can be recycled together (plastic + electronics)

Battery: N/A (USB-powered, no battery to dispose)
