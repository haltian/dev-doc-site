Thingsee POWERCOVER

Power outlet monitoring sensor for tracking energy consumption and device status

Overview

Thingsee POWERCOVER is a smart sensor designed to monitor power outlets, tracking energy consumption and device operational status. It provides insights into power usage patterns without complex installation.

Key Features

  • Power monitoring - Track energy consumption at outlet level
  • Device status - Detect when devices are on/off or in standby
  • Non-invasive installation - Easy attachment to existing outlets
  • Wireless reporting - Real-time data transmission

Primary Use Cases

Energy Management

  • Office equipment power tracking
  • Appliance energy monitoring
  • Standby power identification

Facility Operations

  • Equipment runtime monitoring
  • Usage pattern analysis
  • Maintenance scheduling based on usage

Sustainability

  • Energy waste identification
  • Carbon footprint tracking
  • Efficiency improvement insights
